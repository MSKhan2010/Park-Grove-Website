Our Story

Providing quality care with dignity and respect since 1985.

A Legacy of Care

Park Grove was established in July 1985 and has been under the ownership of Kevin & Una Banks since March 1997, who have over 30 years of experience working in the care sector.

We are committed to maintaining and improving the quality of our service. In January 2006 we were awarded the Investors in People award. We maintain a comprehensive quality policies and procedures manual, constantly under review and revision. A summary of our most recent inspection report is available at CQC online. As of 2026, Park Grove remains under Crown Care Services.

Comfort & Facilities

Registered for 32 residents, our rooms span the ground and first floors (serviced by a lift). We offer ample bathrooms, including a wet room and an easy walk-in modern bath, all equipped with grab rails for wheelchair and zimmer frame accessibility.

A Nurse call facility is in every room. We feature two dining rooms and four lounges (Sky TV in two). A touchscreen computer is accessible for residents to keep in touch with family via Skype, Facebook, or email, alongside free Wi-Fi throughout the home.

Community & Lifestyle

Located on the main A59, we are within a 5-minute walk of village shops, the post office, bank, and library. Excellent transport links via bus and train make visiting easy.

Residents enjoy a vibrant programme of events arranged within the home. Local amenities include a sports centre, parks, a cricket ground, and OAP clubs with weekly bingo and seasonal fayres. Local churches of various denominations frequently visit the home for those wishing to join in communion.

Make It Your Own

You are most welcome to bring any personal belongings to the home. Smaller items such as a special chair, radio, TV, ornaments, shelving, and pictures can be brought in, and if you would like your room decor to change, this will be done.

If you would like to bring in larger items (e.g., bed, dressing table, or wardrobe), we will do everything we can to accommodate you and personalise your environment to truly make it feel like home.
