The wider team

The people who make each day feel cared for.

From senior carers to kitchen, housekeeping and laundry support, the Park Grove team works together to keep residents comfortable, safe, nourished and respected.

Senior Staff

Qualified senior support

Pauline Hart, Janice Molyneux and Clare Halton work on a shift rota and are all NVQ Level 3 qualified. Seniors are responsible for medication administration and also cover on-call duties.

Care Assistants

Daily care with warmth

Sixteen care assistants support residents at different times of the day. They are dedicated to their roles and help deliver an excellent care programme across the home.

Housekeeping

Fresh, calm surroundings

Park Grove takes pride in cleanliness as well as care. Domestics work Monday to Friday to keep the home spotless, with laundry support for bed linen and personal items.

Kitchen

Nutritious, thoughtful meals

Three cooks provide varied, nutritional meals and look after individual dietary needs. Kitchen assistants help maintain the high food standards residents enjoy each day.

Training

Always developing

Training is treated as an ongoing part of the culture, helping staff refresh skills, discuss best practice and continue improving the quality of support residents receive.
